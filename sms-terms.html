Program Name

Rosalia Group SMS Program

Description

The Rosalia Group SMS Program sends text messages related to tour confirmations, appointment reminders, and apartment inquiry follow-ups for properties managed by Rosalia Group.

Opt-In

By checking the SMS consent box on the booking form at book.rosaliagroup.com, you agree to receive SMS messages from Rosalia Group. Consent is not a condition of purchase or service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per booking for appointment confirmation, reminders, and follow-up.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to cancel. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.
