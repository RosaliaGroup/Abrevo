About This Home

422 Faitoute Ave
Roselle Park, NJ 07204

This beautifully renovated colonial in the heart of Roselle Park offers the perfect blend of classic charm and modern updates. Featuring hardwood floors throughout, an updated kitchen with stainless steel appliances and pantry, and 1.5 bathrooms across three bedrooms.

The full basement provides ample storage and additional living potential. Step outside to an enclosed front porch, a large fenced backyard with a spacious deck and garden area — perfect for entertaining. Walking distance to NJ Transit and downtown Roselle Park.
