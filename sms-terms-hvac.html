Program Name

Mechanical Enterprise LLC SMS Program

Description

The Mechanical Enterprise LLC SMS Program sends text messages related to HVAC appointment confirmations, technician dispatch notifications, and service reminders for NJ homeowners.

Opt-In

By checking the SMS consent box on the booking form at book.mechanicalenterprise.com/hvac, you agree to receive SMS messages from Mechanical Enterprise LLC. Consent is not a condition of purchase or service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per appointment for confirmation, technician updates, and service reminders.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to cancel. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.
